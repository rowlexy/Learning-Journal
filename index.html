My new journey as a software developer

After several months of learning in the frontend Developer Career Path, I have made the big jump to also dive into the backend developer Career Path. I am committed to dedicating my time and resources to acquire the required skills and knowledge needed to excel in this role.

How I stay committed to learning

I like to think of myself as a lifelong learner. I used to spend lots of hours learning, then try to create simple projects using what I learned or work new techniques into existing projects

While that was fun, I felt like it would be helpful to share what I was learning and most things about my journey with the world.

How I got started

I started simple and gradually grew my learning journal site. I would take notes about what I was learning. After each learning session, I would use my notes to not only reflect on what I learned but also write short summaries of what I learnedusing my own words.

That helped me learn at my pace and I quite understood every lesson. I also realized that posting my learning summaries was also helping others learn and stay motivated.
